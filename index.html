About Us

Trusted Diamond Experience in Bangkok

Noble Gems International Co.,Ltd was incorporated in 2005. The founder has been working in the diamond industry since 2001, with the family having around 50 years of experience in the diamond trade.

We focus on natural diamonds, direct communication, trusted sourcing, and long-term client relationships. We assist clients looking for loose diamonds across a wide range of shapes, sizes, and color grades.
