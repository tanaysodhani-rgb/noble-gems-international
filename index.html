About Noble Gems

Bangkok diamond sourcing built on trust and experience.

Noble Gems International Co., Ltd. was incorporated in 2005 in Bangkok. The business is built on decades of family experience in the diamond trade, with direct client communication and long-term trust at the center of every relationship.

From our Bangkok office at Bangkok Gems and Jewelry Tower, we assist clients with natural diamonds across different shapes, sizes, colors, clarities, and certification requirements.
