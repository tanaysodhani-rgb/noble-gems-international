About Us

Bangkok diamond sourcing with experience, care, and discretion.

Noble Gems International Co., Ltd. was incorporated in 2005 in Bangkok. The business is supported by decades of family experience in the diamond trade and a practical understanding of what clients need when sourcing stones.

From our office at Surawongwattanakarn Tower (Bangkok Gems and Jewelry Tower), we assist clients with natural diamonds across different shapes, sizes, colors, clarities, and certification preferences. Our approach is simple: understand the requirement, communicate clearly, and source with care.

Diamond Range

Natural diamonds in selected shapes, sizes, colors, and clarities.

Round Diamonds

Round brilliant diamonds are available across different sizes, colors, clarities, and certificate preferences depending on current market availability.

Fancy Shape Diamonds

We source round, oval, pear, emerald, princess, cushion, radiant, marquise, heart, asscher, baguette, trillion, kite, hexagon, and other fancy shapes based on client requirements.

Specific Requirements

Share your preferred shape, carat size, color, clarity, certificate preference, and budget. We can assist with a wide color range including white diamonds, brown diamonds, yellow diamonds, black diamonds, and other specific color requests depending on availability.
