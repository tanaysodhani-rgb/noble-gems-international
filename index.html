About Noble Gems

Family experience in gems, diamonds, and jewellery.

Noble Gems International Co., Ltd. was incorporated in 2005 in Bangkok. The business is built on decades of family experience in the diamond and jewellery trade, with direct client communication and long-term trust at the center of every relationship.

From our Bangkok office at Bangkok Gems & Jewellery Tower, we assist clients with diamonds, colored stones, pearls, women’s fine jewellery, wedding jewellery, heavy jewellery, and custom sourcing requirements.
